Creating a custom build requires navigating the garage interface and utilizing the save system. Unsaved modifications will be lost if your vehicle is despawned or if you leave the server. Follow this step-by-step process to ensure your custom builds are permanently saved.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Garage Interface">
    Drive your vehicle into the designated garage zone or open the customization menu from your player HUD to begin editing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Paint and Materials">
    Navigate to the material tab and apply your primary and secondary paint finishes. Choose between matte, metallic, gloss, or chrome.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Custom Rims">
    Browse the rims menu and select a design that matches your vehicle class. Ensure the stance fits your drag racing preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save Your Custom Preset">
    Open the save menu within the garage interface, choose an empty slot, and save your preset to secure your modifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Managing your saved presets allows you to quickly swap between different setups depending on whether you are drag racing, roleplaying, or engaging in police pursuits.

| Preset Feature | Menu Command | Practical Application |
| **Save Preset** | Save Build | Saves current paint, rims, and material configurations |
| **Load Preset** | Load Build | Spawns your saved custom vehicle instantly |
| **Reset Vehicle** | Clear Customization | Reverts the vehicle back to its factory stock settings |

<Section icon={Target} title="Prop Spawner and Drag Mode Setup">

To test the builds you create in the drag drive simulator garage, you can use the newly added Save, Load, & Drag Mode Prop Spawner. This system allows players to construct custom drag strips and staging areas anywhere on the map, facilitating organized community races.

Setting up a proper drag strip requires placing staging props and utilizing private server commands to ensure fair starts.

| Tool / Command | Action | Usage Scenario |
| **Prop Spawner Menu** | Spawns track barriers | Outlines the boundaries of your custom drag strip |
| **Drag Mode Prop** | Places staging lights | Aligns vehicles at the starting line |
| **!cd [x]** | Initiates countdown | Synchronizes the start of the race for all drivers |

When hosting a drag race, use the countdown command by typing `!cd 5` in the chat to initiate a five-second visual countdown on the screen. This eliminates starting line confusion and ensures that races are decided by vehicle tuning and reaction time rather than connection latency.

<Callout type="info" title="Command Info">
