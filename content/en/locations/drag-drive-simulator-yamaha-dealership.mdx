Once you have secured your bike from the **drag drive simulator yamaha dealership**, you must visit the tuning menu to unlock its true potential. The gear ratio system is the heart of performance in this simulator. A "Short Ratio" setup will provide a violent launch but will hit the rev limiter too early, while a "Long Ratio" setup might have a higher top speed but will be sluggish off the starting line.

For bikes like the Aerox, a balanced approach is best. You want enough torque to get off the line without excessive wheelie-ing, but enough top-end to finish the race at the Sentul Track before your opponent catches up.
