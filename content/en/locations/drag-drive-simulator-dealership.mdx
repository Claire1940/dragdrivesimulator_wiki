<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Customization & Performance Tuning at Dealerships">

Buying a vehicle is only half the battle; the real performance lies in how you customize and tune it. Many dealerships feature integrated modification garages where you can adjust your vehicle's mechanical and cosmetic settings.

The most critical mechanical system is Gear Ratio Tuning. Adjusting your gear ratios allows you to strike the perfect balance between off-the-line acceleration and top-end speed. Shorter gear ratios improve launch times but cap your maximum speed, making them ideal for short drag strips. Longer gear ratios provide higher top speeds, perfect for long highway runs or the Pettarani Track.

| Upgrade Type | System / Pass | Performance Impact | Price / Access |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gear Tuning** | Base Mechanics | Adjusts acceleration & top speed | Free (Base Game) |
| **Pro Suspension** | Suspension Pro Pass | Enhances damping & stiffness | 29 Robux |
| **Advanced Paint** | Advance Paint Pass | HEX color support & textures | 59 Robux |
| **Custom Plate** | Custom Plate Pass | Custom alphanumeric text plates | 79 Robux |

For players looking to take their customization to the next level, several premium game passes offer advanced options. The Suspension Pro Pass allows you to fine-tune damping and stiffness, while the Advance Paint Pass unlocks HEX color support and premium materials like carbon fiber and matte finishes.

<Callout type="success" title="Tuning Success Rule">
Always test your tuning adjustments in free roam before entering a high-stakes tournament. Small, incremental gear ratio tweaks are much easier to manage than complete overhauls.
</Callout>
