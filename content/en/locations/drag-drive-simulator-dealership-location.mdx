<Section icon={Steps} title="Step-by-Step Navigation Guide">

If you are struggling to find a specific **drag drive simulator dealership location**, follow this optimized route to discover all major showrooms in a single session. This route starts from the main spawn and moves clockwise around the map.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start at Auto's">
    Begin at the central spawn point. This is the **Auto's Car Dealership**. Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the basic inventory and the nearby tuning shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cross to the Parking Lot">
    Simply drive across the main road from the spawn. You will find the **Parking Lot Dealership**, which often contains overflow stock and slightly more specialized land vehicles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Head North to Luxury">
    Follow the main highway north toward the hills. Look for the large glass building; this is the **Supercar Dealership**. Even if you can't afford anything yet, it is a great place to set a waypoint.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="West to the Coastline">
    Drive toward the sunset. Along the beach, you will encounter the **Edison Dealership** (Electric) and the **Aquatic Dealership** (Boats) situated near the pier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="South to the Airport">
    Finally, head to the southernmost point of the map. The **Plane Dealership** is located within the airport perimeter, right next to the quarter-mile drag strip.
  </Step>
</Steps>
