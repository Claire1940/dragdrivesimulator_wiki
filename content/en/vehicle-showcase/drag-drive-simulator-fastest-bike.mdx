<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Expert Gear Ratio Tuning for Maximum Speed">

To unlock the potential of the **drag drive simulator fastest bike**, you must master the gear ratio system. Tuning allows you to sacrifice launch speed for a higher top-end or vice versa. For drag racing, the goal is to reach your maximum possible speed exactly as you cross the finish line, rather than hitting the rev limiter too early.

| Tuning Parameter | Shorter Ratio Effect | Longer Ratio Effect | Recommended Setting |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Final Drive** | Faster off-the-line launch | Higher maximum top speed | Balanced for track length |
| **1st - 2nd Gear** | High torque, easy wheelies | Smoother, controlled start | Slightly shorter for launch |
| **5th - 6th Gear** | Quick shifts, low top end | Long pull, maximum velocity | Long for high-speed tracks |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tuning Tip">
Always test your tuning changes at the **Sentul Track**. If you find your bike hitting the "red line" and stopping its speed increase before the finish line, your gears are too short. Increase the ratio length for your final gear to keep accelerating.
</Callout>

**Advanced Performance Upgrades:**

The **Suspension Pro Pass** (29 Robux) allows for adjustments to damping and stiffness, which prevents the bike from "looping" during high-torque launches. Additionally, the **DragSpec Pass** (109 Robux) unlocks purpose-built machines that have higher base stats than standard dealership versions.
