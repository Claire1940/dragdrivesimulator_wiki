<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Performance and Tuning Logic">

A true **drag drive simulator vehicle showcase** isn't just about looks; it is about what is under the hood. The **Gear Ratio** system is the most critical mechanic for any competitive racer. Tuning allows you to adjust how your engine power is distributed across different speeds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Launch Testing">
    Head to the **Sentul Track** and perform a stock run. Note your 0-60 time and where your engine starts to struggle in the RPM range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjusting Final Drive">
    Shorten your gears for a faster launch if you are spinning your tires too much or take too long to reach peak RPM.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stretching Top End">
    If you hit your rev limiter before the finish line, lengthen your higher gears to increase your maximum achievable speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Suspension Calibration">
    Use the **Pro Suspension Pass** to adjust damping. A stiffer rear setup helps prevent "squatting" during a hard launch, keeping your power on the pavement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Tuning Philosophy">
**Shorter Ratios** = Faster Acceleration / Lower Top Speed. **Longer Ratios** = Slower Acceleration / Higher Top Speed. Finding the "Sweet Spot" for the specific track length is the key to winning.
</Callout>
