The heart of the game lies in its tracks. This **drag drive simulator map tutorial** highlights the two most important locations for competitive players: **Sentul Circuit** and **Pettarani Track**. These areas are where you validate your tuning setups and earn the bulk of your RP through competitive racing.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Sentul Circuit">
    From the main spawn, follow the highway signs toward the outskirts. Sentul is the primary hub for drag racing and is usually the most populated area on any server.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Makassar Expansion">
    Travel through the transition tunnel to reach Makassar City. This area hosts the newer Pettarani Track, which features both road race and drag race systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate a Race">
    Line up at the starting lights. Ensure your tires are warmed up (if applicable) and your gear ratio is set for the specific track length.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Review Performance">
    After the race, check your finish time. If your top speed was reached too early, return to the tuning menu to lengthen your gears.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Different tracks require different strategies. Sentul is a classic drag strip where launch control and reaction time are paramount. Pettarani, however, offers more variety, allowing for road-style racing that tests your vehicle's handling and suspension settings in addition to raw acceleration.

| Track Name | Location | Race Types | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Pettarani Track** | Makassar City | Drag & Road Race | Advanced testing, High stakes |
| **Urban Streets** | Surakarta | Street Racing | Casual cruising, Roleplay |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Tuning Hubs and Customization Shops">

Your map knowledge isn't complete without knowing where to modify your ride. Tuning is the "secret sauce" of this game. A vehicle's stock performance is rarely enough to win at Sentul. You must visit customization hubs to adjust your gear ratios, suspension, and aesthetics.

The tuning system is accessible from most areas, but specific "Stance" and "Paint" shops offer deeper customization. For example, the **Advance Paint Pass** allows for HEX color support and carbon fiber materials, while the **Suspension Pro Pass** gives you control over damping and stiffness—essential for keeping high-power bikes stable on the Pettarani Track.

| Tuning Category | Map Accessibility | Effect on Racing | Recommended Setup |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gear Ratios** | Menu / Free Roam | Acceleration vs Top Speed | Short for Drag, Long for Road |
| **Suspension** | Custom Shops | Stability & Launch | Stiff for Drag, Soft for Cruising |
| **Visuals** | Paint Hubs | Aesthetic / RP | Carbon Fiber for Race Look |
| **Plates** | Custom Shops | Personalization | Unique ID for Roleplay |

<Callout type="tip" title="Tuning Strategy">
When testing a new map route, always start with a balanced gear ratio. If the route has many long straights, like the highway to Surakarta, favor top speed. If the route is short and technical, favor acceleration.
</Callout>
