<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Performance Optimization">

To get the most out of your **drag drive simulator controls**, you must ensure the game runs smoothly. Input lag is the number one enemy of a drag racer. Even a millisecond of delay between your finger press and the in-game action can result in a loss.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Settings Menu">
    Press the **Options button** on your PS4/PS5 controller to bring up the main interface overlay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Graphics Configuration">
    Navigate to the top left of the screen. Use the **Touchpad** to click on the gear icon or the graphics slider.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust for Performance">
    Switch the graphics mode to **Manual**. Lower the bars until the game maintains a stable 60 FPS. This ensures your controller inputs are registered instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Network Stability">
    Check the text chat for any "Server Lag" warnings. If the lobby is stuttering, consider respawning or joining a fresh server.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Performance Gain">
Lowering your graphics settings can reduce input latency by up to 15ms, giving you a competitive edge at the starting line.
</Callout>
